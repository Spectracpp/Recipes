Mashed Cauliflower

Ingredients

  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 pound frozen riced cauliflower
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon cream cheese
  • 1 tablespoon snipped fresh chives, plus more for garnish

Steps

  1. In a 2-quart saucepan, bring chicken broth to a boil over medium high heat. Add frozen riced cauliflower and ground black pepper. Use a spoon to break up the pieces and separate the cauliflower “grains”. Return to a boil and cook, uncovered, about 5 minutes.
  2. Using a mesh sieve, drain the cauliflower well and transfer to a blender. Add butter and cream cheese. Pulse several times, and then purée for about 1 minute.
  3. Stir in chives and place in a serving bowl. Garnish with additional chives, if desired. Serve warm.
